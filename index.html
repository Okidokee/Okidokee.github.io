Directors' Style

Everyone has a favorite director whether they realize it or not. Even if you don't know they made certain movies, odds are you still gravitate to one or more directors' work.

Spielberg

Spielberg's films attract millions of fans of all ages. He's made successful series such as Star Wars, Indiana Jones, and Jurassic Park. These movies have more similarites than you might think. For one they all reside in the same genre, that being action/adventure(with more of an emphasis on adventure). The characters always travel to fascinating lands whether it be a galaxy far away, the Temple of Doom, or a land lost to time.
